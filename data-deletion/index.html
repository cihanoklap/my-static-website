Data Deletion Request

Last updated: January 3, 2026

LiftCurve LLC, doing business as Maple Owl Games ("LiftCurve", "Maple Owl Games", "we", "our", or "us"), respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data from our services.

1. How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data by:

  • Email: Send a request to support@mapleowlgames.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  • In-App: Navigate to Settings > Account > Delete My Data (if available in the game)

Please include the following information in your request to help us locate your data:

  • Your display name or username (if applicable)
  • The email address associated with your account
  • The game(s) you have played
  • Your device type (iOS or Android)

2. What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, we will delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Account information (display name, email, avatar)
  • Game progress and save data
  • In-game purchase history records
  • Leaderboard entries and scores
  • Any user-generated content you submitted
  • Analytics data linked to your account

3. Data We May Retain

Certain data may be retained even after a deletion request for legitimate purposes:

  • Legal compliance: Data required by law, such as transaction records for tax purposes
  • Fraud prevention: Limited data to prevent abuse and protect other users
  • Aggregated data: Anonymous, aggregated statistics that cannot identify you

4. Processing Timeline

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving a verified request. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Please note:

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You will lose all game progress and purchased items
  • If you reinstall the game, you will start fresh as a new user

5. Third-Party Data

Our games use third-party services (such as Google, Facebook, advertising networks, and analytics providers) that may have collected data about you. To request deletion from these services, you may need to contact them directly:

  • Google Privacy Settings
  • Facebook Privacy Center
  • AppLovin Privacy
  • Unity Privacy Policy
